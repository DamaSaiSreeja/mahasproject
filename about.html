Mrs. K. Mahalakshmi Kamalakannan - Founder & Director

Mrs. K. Mahalakshmi Kamalakannan - Founder & Director of our group is the D/o Late.Thiru Isari Velan who was a Cine Artist & also a Deputy Minister in MGR’s Cabinet. She used to accompany her father for his film shootings and used to be fascinated with the art of makeup.This kindled the spark in her to embark on a course in the Beauty Industry.

She did a 3 year National Apprenticeship Certificate Course in Beauty & Hair dressing.etc. She has also started an academy in Malaysia in collaboration with the Govt. of Malaysia.
